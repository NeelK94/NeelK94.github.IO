My journey so far

I graduated with BSc Particle Physics and Cosmology from Lancaster University in 2016.
I spent four years teaching mathematics at a school in East London. Here, I took on a number of responsibilities.
I successfully led a major overhaul in curriculum plans and teaching and learning pedagogy, replacing old practice with evidence-informed, data-driven practice.
This success resulted in a publication and an invitation to present findings at a national conference. In addition, I was awarded the opportunity to conduct further research in Shanghai, which was used to drive improvement in London schools.

With a deep-rooted love for mathematics and analytics, I had a desire to work in an industry where these skills are used on a daily basis to drive world-wide change.
